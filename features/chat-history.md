# Chat history

Beyond saved memories, MemoryPlugin imports your past conversations and keeps them searchable.

## Importing

Import chat history from supported platforms like ChatGPT and Claude. Imports are opt-in - you choose what comes in.

## Searching

Ask a question in plain language and MemoryPlugin finds the earlier exchange where you worked it out. Optional auto-sync keeps new conversations flowing in.
